I teach computer science courses at Warsaw University of Technology and supervise bachelor and master theses in areas related to data science, machine learning, databases, and artificial intelligence.

## Courses

- **Databases**
- **Artificial Intelligence Methods**
- **Object-Oriented Programming**

## Thesis Supervision

I supervise bachelor and master theses in Computer Science, especially topics connected with:

- machine learning and data science,
- time series analysis and prediction,
- anomaly detection,
- natural language processing,
- databases and data engineering,
- process mining and business intelligence.

## Postgraduate Studies

Since August 2024, I have served as **Head of Postgraduate Studies in AI-Powered Business Intelligence** at Warsaw University of Technology. This role includes study programme coordination, teaching organization, student contact, and collaboration with lecturers and practitioners.

## Teaching and Educational Interests

My teaching is focused on connecting computer science foundations with practical data-oriented systems. I am particularly interested in courses and thesis projects where students can combine databases, machine learning, programming, and analytical thinking in realistic technical problems.
